Got Questions? Here's What to Know

Q1: What does this tool actually do?
A: It analyzes your cough sound along with basic symptoms — including age, body temperature, weight, smoking status, and how long the cough has lasted — to assess the likelihood of pulmonary tuberculosis (TB). It then gives a result of either “Probable TB” or “Unlikely TB” to help guide your next steps.
Q2: Can it tell me if I have a disease?
A: It provides a possible condition based on the cough sound and personal info, but it’s not a medical diagnosis. Always see a healthcare professional to confirm.
Q3: Who is this tool for?
A: Anyone can use it — individuals, health workers in remote settings, or researchers running health studies.
Q4: What makes it reliable?
A: It uses trained algorithms that learn from real cough samples. Accuracy improves with clear recordings and accurate patient info.
Q5: Does it track my health over time?
A: Yes, if you use it regularly, you can monitor how your cough is changing, which can help during treatment or recovery.
Q6: Will my data be saved?
A: Your recordings and details are only saved if you choose to. Nothing is stored by default.
